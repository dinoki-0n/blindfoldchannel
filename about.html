About Blindfold Channel

Blindfold Channel It's a indie project created by Dinoki ON, a guy who wants to be known through art and his creations. Dinoki brings you a fantastic and shocking history that will make you get sentimental or maybe a little dramatic.
The history is about a guy who is looking for acceptance but it is very difficult for him find it, since it went through events that shouldn't have happened to him, And now he feels alone and depressive, but nevertheless a group of friends helps him deal with the emotions that this guy brings, and make him at least feel happy and accepted.

Blindfold Channel search in what it is to mix the adaptations of the creator or other cartoonists to capture them and create a series/film in them. Blindfold Channel tell us about a guy who looks for acceptation and facing challenges on life and the virtual community of internet. With the help of the friends, they looks for a better life without destruction and chaos. So Blindfold Channel is the project who want to reach to more people, this project is starting well and starting good B] !
